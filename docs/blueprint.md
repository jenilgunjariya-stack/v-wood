# **App Name**: Timely Finds

## Core Features:

- Clock Catalog Browsing: View a curated collection of clocks with high-quality images, titles, and pricing.
- Individual Clock Detail Views: Access dedicated pages for each clock, showcasing more detailed descriptions, multiple angles, and specifications.
- Shopping Cart Functionality: Add desired clocks to a cart, adjust quantities, and remove items before checkout.
- Simplified Checkout Flow: A user-friendly sequence to capture shipping details and confirm orders, simulating a purchase (no actual payment integration for MVP).
- AI-Powered Style Assistant Tool: Users can describe their interior design preferences in natural language, and the AI tool will suggest matching clock styles or specific clock products.
- Admin Product Listing Management: A basic interface for an administrator to add, edit, and remove clock products.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, deep blue (#354160) to convey professionalism, tradition, and elegance. Chosen for a classic, timeless feel against a light scheme.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue-gray (#F1F3F6), offering a clean and airy canvas that harmonizes with the primary color.
- Accent color: A vibrant, clear teal (#46c7e2), analogous to the primary but brighter and more saturated, providing a modern contrast for call-to-action elements and highlights.
- Headlines font: 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant, high-end feel in titles and key headings.
- Body text font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean lines and excellent readability in product descriptions and general interface text.
- Use a set of clean, minimalist line icons for navigation, product actions (e.g., cart, search), and to represent various clock categories, maintaining a modern and sophisticated aesthetic.
- Employ a responsive, grid-based layout for product display, prioritizing ample white space and clear visual hierarchy to enhance product presentation and user focus across devices.
- Incorporate subtle micro-interactions such as gentle hover effects on product cards and smooth transitions for shopping cart updates, providing polished feedback without being distracting.
